Methodology & Sources

Figures combine FAO production data (1990–2020) and OECD dietary surveys synthesized into a storytelling dataset. Crop values reflect harvest tonnage of wheat, rice, and maize—the trio supplying most of the world's calories. Protein figures depict per capita meat availability, a proxy for consumption.

All calculations are smoothed for clarity while preserving relative rankings. Geographic coordinates anchor each spotlight country within a stylized equirectangular projection. Colors follow a perceptually uniform palette tuned for dark and light modes.
